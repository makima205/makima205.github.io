Frequently Asked Questions

What projects do you undertake?

We specialize in Residential Architecture, Commercial Office Spaces, Urban Design, and Sustainable Landscape planning. No project is too big or too small.

Do you work outside of India?

Yes! CreateSpace is a global infrastructure firm. While our studios are in India, we work with distributed teams and clients worldwide.

What is the estimated timeline?

Timelines vary by scope. An interior project might take 3-4 months, while large architectural builds can take 12-18 months. Contact us for a precise estimate.

How do you charge for services?

We offer both fixed-fee structures and square-footage based pricing, depending on the project type. We provide a transparent quote after our initial consultation.
